Story number and name: 1 - Extend user profile detail with usage 
Story points: 3
Brief description to see the story in action: Navigate to any user's UserDetail page 
by clicking on them in the UserList sidebar. You'll then see the rendered cards if they
have >0 posts - one for Most Recent and another for Most Commented. 

Story number and name: 2 - @mentions in comments
Story points: 6
Brief description to see the story in action: Navigate to any user's photos and write a comment
with the @ character to trigger the suggestions of any user to @. Post your comment to see that
every comment is a link to that user's account. You can also navigate to a user's detail page 
to see photos they are mentioned in. 

Story number and name: 5 - Deleting Comments, Photos, and Users 
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action: 
For photos - click the trash can at the top right corner of your own photo.
For comments - click the trash can at the right side of your own comment. 
For users - press 'Delete Account' on your own User Detail page.
